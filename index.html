This repository contains traces that have been made publicly available through the ATLAS project from our generous collaborators at the Los Alamos National Lab, and Two Sigma.

Please let us know about any issues, insights, or papers you publish using these traces by sending email to the ATLAS project mailing list: info@project-atlas.org.

Please cite the following paper when your publish work that uses any of these traces:
  • George Amvrosiadis, Jun Woo Park, Gregory R. Ganger, Garth A. Gibson, Elisabeth Baseman, Nathan DeBardeleben. "On the diversity of cluster workloads and its impact on research results." In Proceedings of the 2018 USENIX Annual Technical Conference, Boston, MA, July 11-13, 2018.
