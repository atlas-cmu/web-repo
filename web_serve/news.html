Trace Release

The LANL Trinity trace has been released.

It consists of a scheduler log collected from a 9000-node, 300000-core supercomputing cluster, over the course of 3 months in 2017.

Trace Release

The LANL Mustang trace has been released.

It consists of a scheduler log collected from a 1600-node, 30000-core general-purpose cluster, over the course of 5 years from 2011 to 2016.
